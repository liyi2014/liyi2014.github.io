Intro

  • Yi Li received her Ph.D. degree from Business School at Pusan National University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Jangwoo Lee. Before that, she received her Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Interdisciplinary Research Center for Society and Behavior from Donbei University of Finance and Economics in 2011 and 2014, respectively. Her research interests include portfolio management and behavioral finance, with a focus on market anomaly.
